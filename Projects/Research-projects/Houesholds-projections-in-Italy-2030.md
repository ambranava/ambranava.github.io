# Households projections in Italy (2030)  
### Lombardy and Sardinia

## Overview
This project examines demographic change and household structure in two Italian regions, Lombardy and Sardinia, with a focus on:
- projected changes in household composition
- population age structure and ageing dynamics
- regional similarities and differences in demographic trends

The analysis combines quantitative demographic projections with integrated interpretation to explore how population ageing interacts with household and family structures at the regional level.

---

## Research objectives 
The project aims to:
- analyze projected changes in household composition up to 2030 
- examine population age structure as a key demographic variable 
- interpret household dynamics in relation to population ageing
- compare regional demographic trajectories through integrated analytical interpretation

---

## Data sources
- Official demographic statistics (ISTAT)
- Aggregated regional-level demographic data 
- Secondary data sources only  

> Note: no personal or sensitive data were collected or processed

---

## Methodology
The project is based on:
- descriptive demographic analysis  
- scenario-based projections 
- comparative regional analysis

The analysis incorporates:
- regional demographic datasets
- projected household structure data
- population age pyramids by region

Quantitative outputs are presented separately for each region, while comparative insights are developed through integrated interpretation of demographic patterns and projected trends.

## Research scope and limitations
- Regional focus limited to Lombardy and Sardinia  
- Projections based exclusively on available secondary data  
- Results should be interpreted as scenario-based demographic estimates rather than predictive forecasts
