# Appendix C – Quantitative Results

## Purpose of this appendix
This appendix documents the quantitative outputs of the project *High School Students and University Choice*.

Its purpose is to provide transparent access to the empirical results supporting the findings discussed in the main project section, without reproducing the complete analytical report within the portfolio.

---

## Content overview
The quantitative outputs include:
- descriptive analysis of university orientation sources
- factors influencing faculty and university selection
- levels of certainty within the decision-making process
- perceptions and awareness of Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore
- evaluation of institutional communication channels

Results are presented through frequency distributions and descriptive summaries derived from Likert-scale items.

## Methodological note
- Sample size: small and non-representative 
- Data collection method: self-administered paper questionnaire 
- Analytical approach: descriptive and exploratory  

The results should therefore be interpreted as exploratory findings rather than statistically generalizable outcomes.

---

## Language note
The quantitative outputs included in the attached PDF are presented in Italian, as the original study was conducted within the Italian educational context and administered to Italian-speaking participants.

All analytical dimensions, variables, and interpretations are summarized and discussed in English within the main project section. The original Italian-language outputs are included for transparency and methodological documentation purposes.
